KCD Revit Add-in Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Data Collection

The KCD Revit Add-in does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or project data from users. All operations are performed locally within Autodesk Revit, using the Revit API and standard libraries. No user data, project data, or usage analytics are collected or processed by the add-in.

2. Third-Party Data Sharing

The KCD Revit Add-in does not share any data with third parties. No analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs are integrated that would collect or transmit user data. If, in the future, any data sharing is required, all third parties will be contractually obligated to provide equal or greater protection of user data as stated in this policy.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since the add-in does not collect or store any user data, there is no data retention or deletion policy required. No information is retained by the add-in after use.

4. Revoking Consent and Data Deletion

As no data is collected or stored, users do not need to revoke consent or request deletion. If you have questions or concerns regarding privacy, please contact the publisher.
